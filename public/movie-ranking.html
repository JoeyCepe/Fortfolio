The Best Movies According to Joey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Pursuit of Happines

The main message of this movie is that if you believe you can do something and have a goal, stick to it and follow it through. The movie also presents the following values: the importance of doing your best.

A Few Good Men

Examines the clash between duty and morality, the complexities of loyalty, and the consequences of blindly adhering to authority.

3 Idiots

Throughout the story, Rancho is telling his friends that don't chase success. But achieve excellence and success will come to you themselves. But, unfortunately, we only want success and don't focus on excellence because it demands hard work, commitment, and passion.
